WASD for movement
Z: undo
R: restart
Q: interact: When standing on those fancy blocks, press Q to go into that level. Once that level is complete, you'll unlock the next level.
ESC: leave out of a level you've had enough of, without solving it. You can do this in the test world to go to be overworld
TAB: go to alternate, overhead camera (only works in overworld right now, no other levels need an alternative camera)
F: if you want to skip a level, press F while standing on a level instead of Q to 'solve' the level

debug binds:
1: toggle debug mode (able to move around with WASD, place/break blocks with left/right click. 
0: to go back to game mode
3: toggles cheating (you don't fall)
J: toggle to a more friendly FOV for flying around
backspace: reset camera to start position
E: toggle rendering of 'real' models, and the outline shader. not all models have art yet, and the art that is made doesn't work well with the outline shader.
Y: toggle the debug text that's visible in the top left
T: draw camera boundary lines (shows camera chunks in overworld, and level boundary in other levels)

some less interesting debug binds:
C, X, and V handle saving the camera / alt camera
B, and N change camera FOV by 1
M clears all solved levels
P snaps camera to face exactly at the nearest axis
2: enables select mode - you can press on an entity with left click and see some properties
